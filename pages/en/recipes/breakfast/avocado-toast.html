Avocado Toast with Poached Egg

15 min 1 serving 320 kcal Breakfast Vegetarian

An Energizing Start to Your Day

This simple, yet exceptionally tasty and nutritious toast is a perfect option for a wholesome breakfast. Creamy avocado, perfectly runny poached egg yolk, and crispy sourdough bread create an excellent combination of flavors and textures.

Avocado provides healthy fats and fiber, while the egg is an excellent source of protein. This breakfast not only tastes good but also provides energy for a long time, preventing snacking before lunch.

Ingredients

  • Sourdough bread 1 slice
  • Avocado 1/2
  • Egg 1
  • Lemon juice 1 teaspoon
  • Apple cider vinegar 1 teaspoon
  • Sea salt to taste
  • Black pepper to taste
  • Chili flakes (optional) a pinch
  • Microgreens/herbs (optional) for garnish

Instructions

  1. Pour water into a small saucepan and bring to a boil. Add a teaspoon of apple cider vinegar (this helps set the egg white).
  2. When the water is boiling, reduce the heat so it's gently simmering. Crack an egg into a small bowl.
  3. Gently stir the water with a spoon to create a vortex, and carefully pour the egg into the center of the vortex.
  4. Cook the egg for about 3-4 minutes for a perfectly runny yolk.
  5. Meanwhile, toast the slice of bread in a toaster or on a pan.
  6. Cut the avocado in half, remove the pit, and scoop the flesh into a small bowl. Mash with a fork, adding lemon juice, a pinch of salt, and pepper.
  7. Spread the avocado mash onto the toasted bread.
  8. Remove the poached egg from the water using a slotted spoon and dry it on a paper towel.
  9. Place the poached egg on top of the avocado, sprinkle with a pinch of salt, pepper, optionally chili flakes, and garnish with microgreens or herbs.

Tips

  • For a perfect poached egg, use the freshest eggs possible.
  • The avocado should be ripe but not overripe – soft when gently pressed.
  • Instead of regular bread, you can use whole grain or gluten-free bread.
  • Experiment with toppings: cherry tomatoes, arugula, or grana padano will add new flavors.
  • The poached egg can be prepared ahead and stored in cold water for up to 24 hours, reheating before serving by placing it in warm (not hot) water for 1 minute.

Health Benefits

This breakfast is not only delicious but also offers numerous health benefits:

Heart Health

Avocados are rich in monounsaturated fats that support heart health and help reduce bad cholesterol levels.

Brain Power

Eggs contain choline, an important nutrient that supports brain health and memory function.

Vision Support

Both eggs and avocados contain antioxidants like lutein and zeaxanthin that support eye health.

Muscle Recovery

The protein in eggs helps with muscle repair and recovery, making this a great post-workout meal.
